Virtual Swap

A JavaScript app to share preloved items with your friends

Inspiration
In-person clothing swaps have stopped during COVID, so I wanted to create a virtual space where friends can post items that they would like to trade within their friend group. Create a private swap "room", invite friends, post items and indicate interest in others' items.

What I'm proud of
Object oriented JavaScript pattern for fetch requets and keeping track of items.

Features

  • Generate a private event to share with friends
  • Browse available items, see items taken and by whom
